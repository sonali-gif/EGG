Eligibility for Ayushman Card

  • Family income below ‚Çπ5 lakhs per annum (as per SECC 2011 data)
  • Rural families: Those identified in SECC 2011 list
  • Urban workers: Unorganized sector workers (construction, sweepers, plumbers, etc.)
  • No restriction on age (entire family covered)
  • Families without any government benefits eligible
  • Agricultural laborers and landless families
  • Persons with disabilities get priority coverage
  • Automatically eligible if listed in SECC database

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Number (mandatory for registration)
  • Proof of Identity (Any government ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bill, Aadhaar)
  • Family Income Proof (Certificate from Gram Panchayat/Taluk)
  • SECC 2011 List (self-verification)
  • Passport Size Photograph (optional)
  • Mobile Number (for OTP verification)
  • Bank Account Details (for any refunds)

Steps to Get Ayushman Card

1

Check Eligibility

Search your name in SECC 2011 list on official website. Alternatively, check family list on state e-Governance portal.

Check Status: PM-JAY Official Website

2

Visit Enrollment Center

Go to designated enrollment center (district hospital, health center, e-governance center) with Aadhaar and address proof.

3

Register Details

Provide Aadhaar and family income details. Staff will verify eligibility from SECC database and proceed with registration.

4

Get Card

Ayushman card is issued on the spot (digital). Physical golden card sent to registered address within 7-14 days.

5

Use Card

Card valid immediately. Visit empaneled hospital with card for free treatment up to ‚Çπ5 lakh annually.

Important Information

  • Processing Time: Immediate (digital card) + 7-14 days (physical card delivery)
  • Cost: Completely FREE. No registration or annual fees
  • Validity: Lifelong validity (no renewal needed)
  • Coverage Limit: ‚Çπ5 lakh per family per year (resets annually)
  • Family Size: Can have up to 5 family members per card
  • Hospital List: Use card in 24,000+ empaneled government and private hospitals
  • Excluded Procedures: Cosmetic surgery, organ transplants not covered (with some exceptions)
  • Card Usage: Can be used across all states (portable nationwide)

Alternatives & Tips

  • Digital First: Card issued digitally on the spot; physical card takes 2 weeks (request digital copy)
  • Mobile App: Download PM-JAY app to check balance, find hospitals, and manage card
  • Family Addition: Can add new family members anytime by updating records
  • Portability: Use card across all states; not restricted to home state
  • Hospital List: Check empaneled hospital list on PM-JAY website before treatment
  • No Waiting Period: Can use card immediately after issue; no waiting period for covered services
  • Lost Card: Get duplicate digital card from enrollment center (free)
  • Alternative Documentation: If Aadhaar not available, use alternative identity proof
