Eligibility for Aadhaar

  • Any resident of India (not just citizens)
  • Permanent resident - residing in India for 12 months in past 14 months
  • Minor children (with parent/guardian consent)
  • Senior citizens and persons with disabilities
  • No age restriction (from birth to old age)
  • Must provide proof of identity and address
  • Foreign nationals with valid long-term visa eligible

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity (Passport, PAN, Voter ID, Driving License)
  • Proof of Address (Electricity bill, Water bill, Telephone bill, Aadhaar)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Birth Certificate, School Certificate, Passport)
  • Passport Size Photograph (4x6 cm or 35x45mm)
  • Mobile Number (for OTP verification)
  • Email Address (optional but recommended)
  • Self-attested copies of documents

Types of Aadhaar

Regular Aadhaar

12-digit unique number for all residents

Masked Aadhaar

Partial display with first 4 digits hidden for security

eAadhaar

Digital version downloadable from official website as PDF

Aadhaar Lite

For people without proof of address, valid for limited purposes

Mobile Aadhaar

Aadhaar stored on mobile device using UIDAI app

Offline Aadhaar

Encrypted and password-protected digital version

Steps to Enroll for Aadhaar

1

Find UIDAI Center

Locate nearest UIDAI enrollment center at banks, post offices, or authorized facilities. Check online portal for center details.

Find Center: Visit UIDAI Website

2

Gather Documents

Collect proof of identity, address, date of birth, and passport-size photograph. Originals and self-attested copies required.

3

Visit Enrollment Center

Go to the center with all documents and mobile number. No appointment needed for most centers. Provide biometric (fingerprint, iris scan).

4

Complete Enrollment

Operator captures data, takes photograph, and collects biometrics. You'll receive enrollment acknowledgment number (EID).

5

Receive Aadhaar

Aadhaar sent to registered address within 60-90 days. Can download eAadhaar immediately from UIDAI portal using EID.

Download eAadhaar: UIDAI Portal

Important Information

  • Processing Time: 60-90 days for physical Aadhaar card; eAadhaar available instantly
  • Cost: Completely FREE. No fees for enrollment or updates
  • Validity: Lifelong validity. Update any changes within 3 years
  • Security: Your data is secured in UIDAI database. Aadhaar is mandatory for many government benefits
  • Updates: Can update address, name, phone number, and email anytime at UIDAI center
  • Authentication: Two types: online (password) and offline (digital signature)
  • Service Charges: Bank Aadhaar updates may charge ‚Çπ100-200; UIDAI center updates are FREE

Alternatives & Tips

  • Alternative Identity Proofs: Voter ID, PAN Card, Passport, Driving License can be used instead of Aadhaar for some purposes
  • Virtual ID: Use 16-digit Virtual ID (VID) for transactions instead of actual Aadhaar number for privacy
  • Biometric Locking: Lock your biometric data if concerned about unauthorized access
  • Check Status: Use EID or mobile number to check enrollment status on UIDAI portal
  • Address Proof: If address changes frequently, provide letter from local government official
  • Minors: Parent/guardian consent document required; child gets unique number at birth
  • Voluntary: Aadhaar enrollment is technically voluntary, but many services now require it
