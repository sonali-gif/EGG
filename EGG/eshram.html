Eligibility for E-Shram

  • Unorganized sector workers aged 18-59 years
  • Not registered under ESIC, NPS, EPFO
  • Migrant workers and daily wage laborers
  • Self-employed workers and artisans
  • Domestic workers and construction workers
  • Agricultural workers and farm laborers
  • Fishermen and street vendors
  • Indian citizens with Aadhaar number

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Number (mandatory)
  • Aadhaar Card (physical or digital)
  • Valid Mobile Number (for OTP)
  • Bank Account Details (for benefit transfers)
  • Proof of Work/Employment (if available)
  • Passport Size Photograph (optional)
  • Email Address (optional)
  • Self-declaration of worker category

Steps to Register for E-Shram

1

Visit e-Shram Portal

Go to e-Shram official website or mobile app. Registration available online 24/7 from home or CSC center.

Online Registration: E-Shram Portal

2

Enter Aadhaar Number

Enter your Aadhaar number and get OTP on registered mobile number. Verify OTP to proceed with registration.

3

Fill Personal Details

Provide name, date of birth, gender, occupation, and worker category. Self-declaration of employment type required.

4

Bank Account Details

Add bank account details (IFSC, account number) for benefits transfer. Optional but recommended for receiving support.

5

Get E-Shram Number

Instant registration complete. Get 12-digit unique e-Shram number via SMS. Card can be downloaded or ordered.

Important Information

  • Processing Time: Instant - e-Shram number issued immediately after registration
  • Cost: Completely FREE. No registration or annual fees
  • Validity: Lifelong (updated annually to confirm active status)
  • Worker Age: Must be 18-59 years old at time of registration
  • E-Shram Number: 12-digit unique identification number, format UIAN (Unorganized Worker Account Number)
  • Physical Card: Optional card can be ordered (usually free or nominal cost)
  • Annual Renewal: Recommended to update worker status annually to maintain benefits eligibility
  • Mobile App: e-Shram app available on Android and iOS for easy management

Alternatives & Tips

  • Instant Registration: Register online from mobile; no office visit needed
  • SMS Updates: All benefits and updates sent via SMS to registered mobile number
  • CSC Centers: Can register through Common Service Centers (CSC) in villages if no internet access
  • Multiple Jobs: If doing multiple occupations, can register under primary occupation
  • Skill Development: Eligible for various government skill training programs
  • Insurance Benefits: Automatic inclusion in PMJJBY (accident insurance) upon registration
  • Government Schemes: Priority access to government welfare schemes and loans
  • Annual Check: Update status annually to maintain active registration and benefit eligibility
