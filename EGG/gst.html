Steps to Get GST Registration

1

Check Eligibility

Verify your annual turnover and business type. GST mandatory if turnover > ‚Çπ40 lakhs (‚Çπ20 lakhs in NE/special states).

2

Apply Online on GST Portal

Visit GST portal (www.gst.gov.in) and file application using Form REG-01. Part A with basic details, Part B with detailed information.

Online Portal: GST India Portal

3

Upload Documents

Upload self-attested scanned copies of PAN, Aadhar, address proof, and business documents. File size typically 5 MB per document.

4

Submit & Verification

Submit application with declaration. Tax officer may request additional info. Physical verification may be conducted if required.

5

Get GST Certificate

Upon approval, GST certificate and 15-digit GSTIN issued. Downloadable from portal. Can start filing returns within 3 days.

Important Information

  • Processing Time: 5-7 days for automatic approval; 10-15 days with verification
  • Cost: Completely FREE - No registration fees
  • Validity: Lifelong (no renewal) - Only annual returns required
  • GSTIN: 15-digit unique identifier (2-state code + 10-digit PAN + 2-check digit)
  • Return Filing: Monthly GSTR-1 (sales), GSTR-3B (summary), quarterly GSTR-9
  • Threshold Exemption: Below ‚Çπ40 lakhs no GST mandatory (‚Çπ20 lakhs in NE)
  • Composition Scheme: Quarterly returns for businesses < ‚Çπ1.5 crore (simpler compliance)
  • Input Tax Credit: Can claim credit on GST paid on input goods/services

Alternatives & Tips

  • Voluntary Registration: Even if below threshold, can voluntarily register for business credibility
  • Composition Scheme: If turnover < ‚Çπ1.5 crore, choose composition for simplified returns (only quarterly)
  • Digital Signature: E-sign option available for faster processing
  • CA Assistance: Chartered Accountants can file on your behalf with authorization
  • Provisional GSTIN: Issued immediately upon application; final after verification
  • Address Change: Notify GST office within 15 days of any address change
  • Invoice Requirements: Must issue compliant invoices post-registration with GSTIN mentioned
  • Input Credit: Claim input GST only on invoices with valid GSTIN
