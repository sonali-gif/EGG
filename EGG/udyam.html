Steps to Register Udyam

1

Visit Udyam Portal

Go to official Udyam registration website. No registration fee. Self-certification process. Available 24/7.

Online Registration: Udyam Portal

2

Enter Aadhaar & PAN

Provide owner's Aadhaar and business PAN. OTP verification on registered mobile number. Fetch auto-populated details from IT database.

3

Fill Business Details

Enter business name, type, address, sector, and turnover. Declare investment in plant/machinery and equipment as applicable.

4

Self-Declare & Submit

Self-certify information (no need to attach documents). Submit application online. Review all details before final submission.

5

Get Certificate

Instant Udyam certificate issued with unique 12-digit number. Certificate downloadable immediately from portal.

Important Information

  • Processing Time: Instant - Certificate issued immediately online
  • Cost: Completely FREE. No registration fees or hidden charges
  • Validity: Lifelong - No renewal required
  • Udyam Number: Unique 12-digit identifier (e.g., UDY123456789012)
  • Replacement for: Supersedes Udyog Aadhaar system (old system discontinued)
  • Self-Certification: No document submission at registration; records kept for verification
  • Amendment: Can update information anytime on portal (free)
  • Certificate: Available in English and Hindi; digitally signed
