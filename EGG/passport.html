Eligibility for Passport

  • Indian citizen by birth or naturalization
  • Minimum age: Anyone can apply (infants require parent application)
  • Resident of India for at least 1 year prior to application
  • No criminal record (convicted prisoners ineligible for 5 years)
  • Not a declared fugitive
  • No pending legal proceedings or debts
  • Persons with financial liabilities may face delays
  • Not involved in terrorism or anti-national activities

Documents Required

  • Proof of Indian Citizenship (Birth Certificate, School Certificate)
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN, Driving License)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bill, Aadhaar, Lease agreement)
  • Date of Birth Proof (Birth Certificate, School Certificate)
  • Passport Size Photograph (4x6 cm, recent, color)
  • Police Verification Form (will be filled during process)
  • Completed Passport Application Form (Form Part A & B)
  • Self-attested copies of all documents

Types of Passports

Ordinary Passport

Regular 36-page blue passport for travel (10 years validity)

Official Passport

White passport for government officials on official duty

Diplomatic Passport

Maroon passport for diplomats and senior government officials

Children's Passport

36-page passport for children (5 years validity)

Senior Citizen Passport

36-page passport for citizens 60+ (5 years validity)

Re-issued Passport

When original is lost, damaged, or pages full

Steps to Obtain Passport

1

Online Registration

Visit Passport Seva Portal and create account. Fill online application form (Part A & B). Schedule appointment slot.

Online Portal: Passport Seva Portal

2

Submit Documents

Attend appointment at PSK (Passport Seva Kendra) with original and attested copies of all documents. Biometric data collected.

3

Police Verification

Police conducts verification at your address within 2-3 weeks. Official will verify identity and residence.

4

Clearance Check

Ministry of External Affairs conducts final security and background checks. Takes 1-2 weeks for normal applicants.

5

Collect Passport

Receive notification by email/SMS when passport is ready. Collect from PSK or opt for courier delivery (paid service).

Important Information

  • Processing Time - Normal: 10-15 days from date of police verification completion
  • Processing Time - Tatkaal: 1-3 days (for urgent travel, ₹2000 additional charge)
  • Validity: 10 years for adults (18-60 years), 5 years for children and seniors
  • Passport Cost: ₹1500 (Regular) + ₹200 (Police verification) = ₹1700
  • Tatkaal Premium: ₹2000 additional for urgent processing
  • Pages: 36 pages (can extend with additional 36-page booklet)
  • Appointment Slots: Book online; walk-ins not accepted at most PSK centers
  • Courier Service: Available for additional ₹500-1000 for delivery at home

Alternatives & Tips

  • Tatkaal Facility: If urgent travel needed, apply via Tatkaal scheme (extra fee applies)
  • e-Passport: Modern passports are electronic with embedded chip for security
  • Renewal: Can be renewed online (lifelong) if previously issued valid passport
  • Address Change: Can update address in passport; requires Form 5 and documents
  • Police Verification: Fastest online through PSP system; mail-based is slower
  • Courier Delivery: Saves time - passport delivered to your home address
  • Lost Passport: Report to police immediately; applying for reissue adds 2-3 weeks
  • Minors: Parents can apply; minor's consent/presence usually required
