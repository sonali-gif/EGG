Aadhaar Card Guide

Eligibility Criteria

  • Any resident of India is eligible if they have lived in India for 182 days or more in the last 12 months
  • No minimum age (Baal Aadhaar)
  • NRIs with valid Indian passport
  • Parent's Aadhaar required (children under 5)

Aadhaar enrolment is completely free.


Processing Time: 90 days for new enrollment

Validity: Lifetime validity, no renewal required

Aadhaar Number: 12-digit unique identification number

Uses: Banking, mobile connection, government services, subsidies

Update: Address and other details can be updated online
