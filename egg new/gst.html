GST Registration Guide

Eligibility for GST Registration

  • Business turnover exceeds prescribed limit
  • Inter-state supply of goods or services
  • E-commerce seller or service provider
  • Mandatory for government tenders
  • Required to claim input tax credit
  • Casual taxable person

Required Documents

Types of GST Registration

Regular GST

Standard registration for businesses above threshold turnover

Composition Scheme

Simplified scheme for businesses < ₹1.5 crore turnover

E-Commerce Operator

Special registration for online sellers and platforms

Casual Taxable Person

For occasional supply of goods/services

Non-Resident Taxable Person

For foreign businesses supplying to India

Input Service Distributor

For entities distributing credit to branches

Steps to Get GST Registration

1

Check Eligibility

Verify your annual turnover and business type. GST mandatory if turnover exceeds prescribed limit.

2

Apply Online

Visit www.gst.gov.in and apply using Form REG-01 (Part A & Part B).

3

Upload Documents

Upload PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, and business documents (max 5 MB per file).

4

Submit & Verification

Application verified by tax officer. Additional details may be requested.

5

Get Certificate

GST certificate and 15-digit GSTIN issued after approval.

Important Information

Processing Time: 5-7 days for automatic approval; 10-15 days with verification

Cost: Completely FREE - No registration fees

Validity: Lifelong (no renewal) - Only annual returns required

GSTIN: 15-digit unique identifier (2-state code + 10-digit PAN + 2-check digit)

Return Filing: Monthly GSTR-1 (sales), GSTR-3B (summary), quarterly GSTR-9

Threshold Exemption: Below ₹40 lakhs no GST mandatory (₹20 lakhs in NE)

Composition Scheme: Quarterly returns for businesses < ₹1.5 crore (simpler compliance)

Input Tax Credit: Can claim credit on GST paid on input goods/services

Alternatives & Tips

Voluntary Registration: Even if below threshold, can voluntarily register for business credibility

Composition Scheme: If turnover < ₹1.5 crore, choose composition for simplified returns (only quarterly)

Digital Signature: E-sign option available for faster processing

CA Assistance: Chartered Accountants can file on your behalf with authorization

Provisional GSTIN: Issued immediately upon application; final after verification

Address Change: Notify GST office within 15 days of any address change

Invoice Requirements: Must issue compliant invoices post-registration with GSTIN mentioned

Input Credit: Claim input GST only on invoices with valid GSTIN
