Indian Passport

Eligibility

  • You are a citizen of India
  • You have proof of Indian citizenship
  • Minor (<18 years) – must be applied by parent/guardian
  • Adult (≥18 years) – can apply on own
  • No minimum age for a minor, but guardian must apply

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online Application)

1

Open Passport Seva Portal

https://portal1.passportindia.gov.in
2

Register / Login

Register as new user or login if already registered

3

Apply for Fresh / Re-issue Passport

4

Fill Personal Details

  • Name, DOB, Gender
  • Address
  • Parents’ details (for minors)
  • Contact info (mobile/email)
5

Choose Passport Type

Normal / Tatkal / Jumbo

6

Upload Documents

As per requirements, all self-attested PDFs

7

Pay Fees Online

8

Schedule Appointment

At Passport Seva Kendra (PSK)

9

Visit PSK

Carry original documents for verification & biometrics

10

Receive Acknowledgement

Receipt after verification; passport delivered by post

Step-by-Step (Offline Application)

1

Visit PSK / RPO

2

Fill Form Carefully in BLOCK LETTERS

3

Attach Passport-size Photograph

4

Attach Self-Attested Documents

  • Proof of Identity
  • Proof of Address
  • Proof of Date of Birth
  • Parent Documents (for minors)
5

Submit Form

6

Pay Fees

Cash or Card

7

Receive Passport

Delivered by post to your address

Passport Fees (India)

Type Fee (₹)
Normal Passport (36 pages) 1,500
Normal Passport (60 pages) 2,000
Tatkal Passport (36 pages) 3,500
Tatkal Passport (60 pages) 4,500
