GST Registration & Certificate

Eligibility

  • Businesses with turnover ≥ ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for NE & special states)
  • Businesses selling goods/services across states (Interstate Supply)
  • E-commerce operators & marketplace facilitators
  • Casual taxable persons (temporary business)
  • Voluntary registration (below threshold)

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online – Recommended)

1

Open GST Portal

Go to https://www.gst.gov.in

2

New Registration

Click Services → Registration → New Registration

3

Fill Basic Details

  • Name of business / proprietor / company
  • PAN of business / proprietor / company
  • Email & Mobile Number (OTP verified)
4

Fill GST Application Form Part B

  • Business details & address proof
  • Bank account details
  • Business constitution / partners / directors
  • Upload required documents (PDF format)
5

Submit & Verification

GST officer verifies application; download GST certificate PDF once approved

Step-by-Step (Offline – Rare)

1

Collect Form GST REG-01

Download: GST Form Download

2

Fill Form & Attach Documents

  • PAN Card
  • Address Proof
  • Business registration proof
  • Bank account proof
3

Submit at State GST Office

Officer verifies & issues GSTIN & certificate
