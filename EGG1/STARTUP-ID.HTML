Startup Registration & DPIIT Recognition (Startup ID)

Who Can Register

  • Innovative, scalable business ideas
  • Less than 10 years since incorporation
  • Annual turnover under ₹100 crore
  • Entity registered as Private Ltd/LLP/Partnership

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online – Startup India / MCA)

1

Incorporate Your Company

Register as Private Limited / LLP / Partnership on MCA portal.

Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)

2

Obtain PAN & TAN

Apply for PAN for the company and TAN if required.

3

Create Startup India Account

Register at Startup India portal and apply for recognition.

Startup India

4

Prepare Document Pack

  • Incorporation certificate
  • MOA/AOA
  • PAN, address proof
  • Brief business plan/overview
5

Apply for DPIIT Recognition

Submit details on Startup India; upload required documents and declaration.

6

Wait for Verification

DPIIT reviews application and issues recognition certificate.

7

Receive Startup ID

Once approved, you will receive a Startup Recognition number/ID.

Step-by-Step (Offline – Paper Preparation)

1

Collect Documents

Obtain printed copies of INC certificates, MOA/AOA, PAN.

2

Prepare Business Overview

Write a concise summary of innovation and scalability.

3

Submit to Incubators / Mentor (Optional)

Get mentorship letters or support documents to strengthen application.

4

Keep Records

Maintain all signed & attested documents for verification.
