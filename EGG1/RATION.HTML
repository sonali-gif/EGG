Ration Card – India

Eligibility Criteria

  • Citizen of India
  • Resident of the applying state
  • No existing ration card in another state
  • Category: APL / BPL / AAY / PHH

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online Application – State Wise)

1

Open Ration Card Portal

Ration cards are issued by State Governments. Visit the National Food Security Act portal and select your state.

Visit NFSA Portal
2

Select Your State Website

On the NFSA website, choose your state to open the Food & Civil Supplies portal. Each state has its own application system.

3

Click on New Ration Card Application

Look for options like:

  • Apply for New Ration Card
  • NFSA Citizen Services
  • Food Security Services
4

Login / Register Using Aadhaar

Register using Aadhaar number of the Head of Family and verify using OTP sent to the linked mobile number.

5

Enter Head of Family Details

Fill details carefully as per Aadhaar:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Mobile Number
6

Add Family Member Information

Add details of all family members including Aadhaar number, age, and relationship with the head of family.

7

Enter Address & Select Ration Category

Enter full residential address and select ration card type: APL / BPL / AAY / PHH (NFSA).

8

Upload Required Documents

Upload clear scanned copies of Aadhaar cards, address proof, income certificate (if applicable), and photograph.

9

Submit Application & Note Reference Number

After submission, you will receive an application/reference number. Save it for tracking your ration card status.

10

Verification & Ration Card Issued

Local Food Inspector may verify details. Once approved, digital or physical ration card is issued.

Step-by-Step (Offline Application)

1

Visit Nearest Office

Visit Fair Price Shop (Ration Shop), Food & Civil Supplies Office, Tehsil Office, or Gram Panchayat.

2

Collect Ration Card Application Form

Ask for the New Ration Card application form from the office counter.

3

Fill the Form Carefully

Fill the form in BLOCK LETTERS with correct personal, family, and address details.

4

Attach Required Documents

Attach self-attested copies of Aadhaar cards, address proof, income certificate (if applicable), and photographs.

5

Submit Application

Submit the completed form at the same office and collect acknowledgement slip.

6

Verification Process

Local authority may conduct document or home verification before approval.

7

Ration Card Issued

After approval, ration card is either sent by post or collected from the office.
