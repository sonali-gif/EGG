Driving Licence (LL & DL – India)

Eligibility

  • Learner’s Licence: 16+ for motorcycles (<50cc), 18+ for cars/other vehicles
  • Permanent Licence: 18+ for cars/motorcycles, 20+ for commercial vehicles
  • Must be an Indian citizen
  • Must hold Learner’s Licence for minimum 30 days (for Permanent Licence)
  • Must pass driving test (for Permanent Licence)

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online Application)

1

Open Parivahan Sewa

Visit https://parivahan.gov.in/parivahan/

2

Select Service

Choose "Driving Licence Related Services" → Your State

3

Login / Register

Use Aadhaar or create an account

4

Select Licence Type

Apply for Learner’s Licence or Permanent Licence

5

Fill Form & Upload Documents

Provide Name, DOB, Address, Vehicle Type, and upload required documents

6

Pay Fees & Book Slot

Pay online and book slot for Driving Test (Permanent Licence)

7

Submit & Receive Licence

Learner’s Licence: few days
Permanent Licence: after test & verification

Step-by-Step (Offline Application)

1

Collect Forms from RTO

Form 1 (Application) & Form 1-A (Medical Certificate if required)

2

Fill in BLOCK LETTERS

3

Attach Documents & Photos

POI, POA, Age proof, Passport-size photos, Medical certificate (if needed)

4

Submit Forms & Pay Fees

Submit at RTO and pay applicable fees

5

Collect Licence

Learner’s Licence: immediately/few days
Permanent Licence: after Driving Test & Verification

Driving Licence Fees (Approx.)

Type Fee
Learner’s Licence ₹200–300
Permanent Licence (Light Vehicle) ₹300–500
Permanent Licence (Commercial Vehicle) ₹1000+
Duplicate / Renewal ₹200–300
