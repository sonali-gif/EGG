Voter ID (EPIC – Electoral Photo ID Card)

Eligibility

  • Indian citizen
  • 18 years or older on or before 1st January of the year
  • Resident of the constituency where you want to register
  • Minors under 18 cannot apply

Documents Required

Step-by-Step (Online)

1

Open NVSP Website

https://www.nvsp.in

2

Login / Register

Using Aadhaar or mobile number

3

Apply Online

Select “Apply online for Voter ID / Form 6” (New Voter Registration)

4

Fill Details

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Address (current residence)
  • Mobile Number & Email ID
5

Upload Documents

  • Proof of Age
  • Proof of Address
  • Photo (if asked)
6

Submit Form

Receive Acknowledgement / Reference ID

7

Verification

Booth Level Officer (BLO) may verify documents at your home

8

Receive Voter ID

Card delivered to your address by post (Typical timeline: 2–4 weeks)

Step-by-Step (Offline)

1

Collect Form 6

From Local Electoral Office / BLO or download:

Download Form 6 PDF
2

Fill Form in BLOCK LETTERS

Include all personal details, address, DOB, and photo

3

Attach Self-Attested Documents

  • Proof of Age
  • Proof of Address
  • Passport size photo (if required)
4

Submit Form

Submit at local Electoral Office / BLO

5

Receive PAN

  • Collect Acknowledgement Slip
  • BLO may visit for home verification
  • Voter ID card sent by post

Voter ID Fees

FREE of cost in India
