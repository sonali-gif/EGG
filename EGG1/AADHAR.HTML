Aadhaar Card

Eligibility Criteria

Any resident of India is eligible if they have lived in India for 182 days or more in the last 12 months.

  • No minimum age (Baal Aadhaar)
  • NRIs with valid Indian passport
  • Parent’s Aadhaar required (children under 5)
